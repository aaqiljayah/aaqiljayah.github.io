Sepak Takraw

First off, what is Sepak Takraw? Sepak Takraw is a foot volleyball game where players touch as well handle the ball using only their feet, knee, chest or head to kick the ball past the net to the opposition side. Failing to kick the ball past the net results in a point to the opposition and the first team scoring the maximum point is declared the winner. Apart from failing to kick the ball past the net, there are other reasons that can lead in a point being awarded to the opposing team.The rules and regulations of the games are very much similar to that of volleyball. A very popular game around Southeast Asia, the game is played using a rattan or synthetic plasitc ball.
Sepak Takraw was originated in the border of Thailand and Malaysia in the 15th century and by the 16th century, the game was spread throughout Southeast Asia. Since 1990, Sepak takraw is also included in the Asian games.
I am currently an active senior member of the Naga Takraw Club and has participated in the Sepak Takraw National tournament in Sri Lanka representing both Naga Takraw Club and my former club, Colombo Malay Cricket Club.
I was also selected as a member of the Srilankan Sepak Takraw National Team to represent the country in the 35th King's Cup 2022 Sepak Takraw World Championship, which is annual event held in Thailand and is considered to be the most prestigious event in the sport. I am yet an active member of the Sri Lankan National Team and am looking forward to all the upcoming tournaments.
